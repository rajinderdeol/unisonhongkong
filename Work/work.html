Our Work for Our Community

Major Focus

We believe people are born EQUAL, where no one should be excluded from the equal social participation due to differences in race, culture, language, economic or social status.

We are endeavour in helping the local ethnic minority communicate with 3 major focuses. In integrating into the Hong Kong society, shouldering the social responsibilities, and having access to equal education, employment, legal, housing, social and medical services, and any rights that Hong Kong residents are entitled.

1. PUBLIC ADVOCACY

After years of evolution, Hong Kong Unison now puts most of our effort in Policy Advocacy work. That includes advocating for better Chinese language education policies for ethnic minorities, expanding ethnic minority students´ post secondary options in education, and fighting for equal access to public services. Our advocacy involves:

  • Education
  • Race Discrimination Ordinance
  • Police Power
  • Labour
  • Equal access to public services

2. PUBLIC EDUCATION

Unison believes,the promotion of multi-culture education could reinforce the racial awareness and sensitivity. It also facilitates the acceptance among different races, and mutual appreciation with respect. In long term, it could benefit the Inclusive Education that align with the goal of EDB’s new policy. In response to the new policy, we kicked off an innovative project in 2005, Racial Harmony @ School to promote racial harmony message at school. It gave Chinese students’ exposure to Ethnic Minorities culture in HK, which helped to promote greater cross-cultural understanding and create a racial inclusive environment in school.

3. EM PROGRAM & SERVICES

In this program, we helped EM students to have earlier goals setting in their study and career path. With more concrete target, their learning incentive will be increased. We organized a series of workshops and talks on career planning and job hunting in secondary schools. By providing latest information about study and career, students have stronger confidence in preparing their life in this competitive society.

Hong Kong Unison is a non-governmental organization founded in March 2001 and recognized as a public charitable institution (IR File No. 91/7763) in 2005.
